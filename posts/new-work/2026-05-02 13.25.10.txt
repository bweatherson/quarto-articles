There are six possibilities:

- $x_1, x_2, x_3, y_1, y_2, y_3$

Here are the refinement relations:

- $x_2$ and $x_3$ are distinct refinements of $x_1$; they are not refinements of each other
- $y_2$ and $y_3$ are distinct refinements of $y_1$; they are not refinements of each other

Here are the $R$ relations

- $x_1$ can $R$-access each of the $y_i$
- $x_2Ry_2$
- $x_3Ry_3$

There is only one propositional variable, $p$, and it is true at $y_2$ and no where else.
